About Me

I am a PhD student at Purdue University working under the superbvision™ of Prof. Saugata Basu and Prof. Elena Grigorescu. My interests are centered in algebraic geometry (and connected areas), and some of its interactions with theoretical computer science, especially complexity theory.

Earlier, I got a masters degree from Brown University, USA, and a bachelors degree from R. V. College of Engineering, Bangalore, India.
